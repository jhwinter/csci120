Job Opportunities

Busboy

Responsibilities:

  • Clean, organize, and arrange dishes in the back of the kitchen
  • Clean up after guests and staff
  • Fill in as a dishwasher during non-service hours
  • Setting and resetting the dining room tables
  • Refill watter and assist servers

Requirements:

  • No dining or serving experience required
  • Food Handler's Card OLCC Certification
  • Positive interpersonal skills required
  • Excellent oral communication skills required

Server

Responsibilities:

  • Take customer orders and deliver it to kitchen staff
  • Serve beverages and food to customers
  • Greet customers at their tables, present menus, and know the specials of the day
  • Present bils to customers and take payment

Requirements:

  • Must be at least 18 years old
  • No dining or serving experience required
  • Food Handler's Card OLCC Certification
  • Positive interpersonal skills required
  • Excellent oral communication skills required
  • Must speak English fluently and be able to write in basic English
  • Good telephone speaking and listening ability
